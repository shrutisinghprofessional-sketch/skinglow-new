How long until I see results?
Most customers report visibly hydrated, plumper skin within 7-10 days. Brighter tone and reduced breakouts typically show by week 2. For pigmentation, fine lines and deep glow, give it the full 8 weeks for best results.
How do I brew Skin Glow Tea?
Add 1 teaspoon (≈2g) of the loose-leaf blend to a cup of freshly boiled water (90°C). Steep for 4-5 minutes, strain and enjoy warm. You can add a drop of honey or a squeeze of lemon to taste. Drink 1 cup daily.
Is it safe to drink every day?
Absolutely. The blend is 100% natural, caffeine-free and free of additives, designed for daily, long-term use. Pregnant or breastfeeding women and those on prescribed medication should consult a doctor first.
